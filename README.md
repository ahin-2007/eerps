Title
Education ERP System
Abstract:
   The Education ERP System is a web-based application developed to automate and manage the academic and administrative activities of an educational institution. The system provides separate portals for administrators, the Head of Department (HOD), faculty members, students, parents, and transport staff with secure role-based access.

Students can access their academic records, attendance, marks, timetable, assignments, study materials, fee details, and important announcements through a single platform. Parents can monitor their child's academic performance, attendance, and receive notifications regarding examinations, events, and announcements. Faculty members can manage student information, record attendance, upload marks, share study materials, and publish notices. The HOD and administrators can monitor departmental activities, generate reports, manage users, and analyze academic performance. The system also supports live bus tracking, real-time notifications, report generation, and centralized data management.

The proposed system reduces paperwork, improves communication between students, parents, and faculty, minimizes manual errors, and provides quick access to academic information. By integrating all educational services into a single platform, the Education ERP System enhances efficiency, transparency, and overall institutional management.



Existing System:
Most educational institutions still rely on manual processes or multiple independent software applications to manage student records, attendance, examination results, announcements, and administrative activities. Data is often maintained using paper records or spreadsheets, resulting in duplication, delays, and increased chances of human error. Students and parents have limited access to academic information, while faculty members spend significant time managing records manually. Communication between the institution and stakeholders is often slow, and there is no centralized platform for managing academic, administrative, and transport-related services efficiently.



Proposed System:
The proposed Education ERP System is a centralized web application that integrates all academic and administrative activities into a single platform. It provides secure login and role-based access for administrators, HODs, faculty members, students, parents, and transport staff. Faculty members can manage attendance, marks, assignments, study materials, and announcements, while students and parents can monitor academic progress in real time. Administrators can manage users, departments, courses, and institutional data, while the HOD can monitor departmental performance and generate analytical reports. The system also includes live bus tracking, real-time notifications, centralized document management, and report generation, making educational administration more efficient, transparent, and reliable.
